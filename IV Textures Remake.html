IV Textures Remake

A port of PC texture mod but this isn’t just a simple port, it is also a fix, correcting messed up textures such as graffiti and signs, bad textures and out-of-place textures. Los Angeles was mostly retextured, the roads around the San Andreas state have been retextured. I also removed some fast-food textures that turned Cluckin’ Bell and Well Stacked Pizza into Burger Shot.

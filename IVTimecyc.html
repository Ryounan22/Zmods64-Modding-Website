SA_ProjectIV Timecyc

A timecyc and colorcycle mod that tries to replicate GTA IV's colors.

Features:

-Extrasunny weathers have lower brightness but more saturation while Sunny weathers have a higher brightness but lower saturation

-SF Sunny and Extrasunny has a bluer tint to give off a colder vibe.

-In desert, the sunny weather has a high brightness and also more saturation to give off a hotter vibe.

-Clouds is more visible in sunny weathers than Extrasunny weathers

-Bright nights so you can see the surrounding pretty clear

-Got rid of pitch black sky at night

-Cloudy, Rainy, Foggy and Sandstorm weathers have a lower brightness

-Fog Fix (AML only)


Updated to 1.2!
